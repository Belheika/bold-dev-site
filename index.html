05 Process

How It
Works

Simple, fast, collaborative. From first message to live site in weeks — not months.

  • 01

    Discovery Call

    We talk about your business, your goals, your audience. 30 minutes is usually enough to understand what you need and what will make your site stand out.

  • 02

    Design & Prototype

    We sketch the layout, choose fonts, define the color palette. You see everything before a single line of code is written. You approve — we build.

  • 03

    Development

    Fast, clean code. Mobile-first, SEO-optimized from day one. No bloated page builders, no drag-and-drop templates — everything is hand-coded for performance.

  • 04

    Launch & Beyond

    We deploy your site, configure analytics, submit to Google. Then we stay close — 12 months of maintenance means if something breaks, we fix it. No questions asked.
